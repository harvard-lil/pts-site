Focus and Privacy

Private Talking Spaces are freestanding, soundproof environments that provide aural privacy to people in libraries, museums, and open offices.

Positioning two or more spaces together creates gravity that draws folks in. The spaces are relocatable to support your dynamic community.

Endless Benefits

Open working environments overexpose us. Too many sounds. Too many distractions. They force us to multi-task and keep us from being our best selves.

When we increase our privacy, we perform at significanly higher levels. We become more creative, our cognitive abilities improve, and our stress levels decrease. Our sick days drop and we add 15% to the bottom line.

The benefits of working in a focused space are clear and long established, but securing the funding and slicing through the red tape of altering your existing physical infrastructure can be challenging.

Fortunately, Private Talking Spaces are a lightweight solution and are installed without alterations to your building's infrastructure. Pop them up in your space and instantly add an environment that encourages focus, happiness, and productivity.
